Welcome Note

Bismillahi Rahmaan Raheem. The entire family of Obafemi Awolowo University Muslim Community welcome you to her online information site. On this site, we share our programmes, islamic values, and news to our brothers and sisters and the public in general. OAU Muslim Community coordinates the affairs of Muslims (students and staff) of our dear institution.

Our Mission

To become better Muslims through ibaadah, dawah and research.

Where We Are

Central Mosque, Religious Centre, Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU), Ile-Ife. OAU, Ile-Ife is located on the map about 4° 40' east of the Prime Meridian or longitude 0 and 7° 30' north of the equator. The town is situated on an elevation of between 800 and 900 feet above sea level. It is fairly plain. Ile-Ife is situated in the tropical zone.
